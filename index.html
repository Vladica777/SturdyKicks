Steps To Take Care Of Your Shoes

By following these three steps - proper cleaning, suitable storage, and wearing proper socks - you'll be well on your way to maintaining clean and fresh sneakers.

Clean

Regularly remove dirt and dust using a soft brush or cloth.

Condition

Apply a suitable shoe conditioner or protectant to keep the material moisturized and protected.

Store

Keep your shoes in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and extreme temperatures.
